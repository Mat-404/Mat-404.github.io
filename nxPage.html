Rocketry Club

I am also a member of the Florida Tech Rocketry Club. I have been working on a few of the parts for our rocket, mainly prototyping and 3D printing.
I was initially using NX and blender to model the parts. Later on, I experimented using other 3D modeling software such as Inventor and settled on Fusion 360.
We found that this software was more user-friendly and had a much easier workflow when it comes to version control.
The bulk of this rocket was 3D printed out of PLA, but the body tube was made out of cardboard and the fins were laser-cut out of birch wood.
